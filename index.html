Instructions

Each node shows its name and its block height. Red nodes are mining nodes.

The status of the blockchain is shown on the bottom. Press T to toggle between displaying blocks by time or by block height.

Press M to toggle between adding non-mining and mining nodes.

Press C to clear graph, press L to enable/disable automatic layout.

Adding nodes

Click to add a node under the cursor position.

Press [ and ] to adjust tendency to connect to closest peers.

Press - and = to adjust number of peers to connect to.

Press number keys 0-9 to adjust radius of peers to connect to.
